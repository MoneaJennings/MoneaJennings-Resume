My name is Monea Jennings and I am certainly a Political Science Major at University of California Riverside. I grew up in Fresno California with my parents, two brothers, and three nephews . I am also a student athlete. I have been a student athlete since the second grade. I have played every sport but when I got to high schoolI settled down with track and field. Sports is a big part of my life because it is who I am and how I live my life. I believe it is important to incorporate sports into everyone's life. Sports build you as a person because it teaches you important elements that you can only get in sports. For example leadership skills, multi-tasking, taking orders, and learning that things do not go your way and you have to keep pushing.

I grew up in an athletic family so sports played a big part in my childhood. Outside of my athletic life I am very active within my community. In high school I was vice president of my senior class, also president of all athletics. I was also part of numerous clubs like Black Student Union, Athletes as readers and leaders, and mock trial. I wanted to keep my leadership and help the community going into college so I joined SAAC, which is the leadership program over all athletics. I am the event coordinator in that. I am also active in the Black Student Union program we have here. Once I graduate with my undergrad I will be going to grad school to get my masters in Political Science. I soon hope to get a job involving sports on a professional level. Some more interesting facts about myself is that I love to bake and go for runs. Since cooking is such a prominent thing in our lives, I found something I like in it so I can enjoy cooking and bake a little bit more. For me running is a stress reliever. It is so calming just to be by yourself in your thoughts and exercise.

In my free time I like to chill at home and be with my dog. I am the type of person to keep my circle small and I do not like big crowds or people so I like to stay to myself. Currently I am not working but I have a lot of volunteer work. I have volunteered at breast cancer walks, libraries, pet centers, etc. My volunteer work is in place of my work experience. The most exciting thing I have done in my life is go skydiving. I believe living with fear is not living at all. The feeling I got when I went skydiving is an unimaginable feeling that I wish upon everyone. Overall I have been living an interesting and boring life.
